  • What information does SAAVI capture about visitors to its web site?
    The www.saavi.com.au web server uses an extended log file format which captures: date and time of visit, referring address (location from which a visitor comes to www.saavi.com.au), type of Internet browser, and visitor's IP address. (Each computer that connects to the Internet is assigned a unique number, an IP address, for identification purposes). The log file does not capture a visitor's email address.
  • Does SAAVI or www.saavi.com.au ask for personal information?
    Several areas of the SAAVI website and the www.saavi.com.au website asks for personal information. This list may be expanded without notice. In the event that it is expanded, additional information may be required. In these areas, your name, address, email address, gender, date of birth and postcode are requested. This information is collected to help us further develop our services, to provide you access to valuable SAAVI and internet-based information and services, and to bill you for those services if you elect to use them.

We use this information to market and improve the web site and our services. We also use this information to send you email notifications about updates to the web site, and to contact you by other means for marketing and other purposes. Our policy is to not give, sell or otherwise distribute the information collected through this site to third parties outside of SAAVI and its subsidiaries (unless required by law); provided, however, in some cases we may use suppliers to assist us in collecting, using or otherwise processing for our benefit the information obtained through this site. Our practice is to require our suppliers to conduct such activities consistent with this policy and our requirements.
  • What is a cookie?
    A cookie is a series of data characters that, when programmed into a mobile app or web site, is placed by the web server into the browser's application folder on your computer. Once placed onto your PC or mobile phone, the cookie will allow the web site or mobile app to "recognise" you as a unique individual.
  • Does SAAVI or the website use cookies?
    Yes. Cookies are used to achieve two goals. The first is to provide SAAVI with the capability to personalise information for certain segments of its customer base. Secondly, in some instances, cookies are used to allow SAAVI the opportunity to associate individual customers with their information profiles.
  • Can cookies be removed from my mobile phone or hard drive?
    Yes, cookies can be removed from your mobile phone or hard drive. Also, depending on what type of web browser and what browser version you are using, you may be able to change the properties on your cookie file so that cookies are not used or saved. Please check with your mobile phone, its browser provider for more information on removing cookies
